Epic Pizza

The finest pizza you ever had

You're gonna love this delicious recipe!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Make a dough ball
  2. Make it flat and round
  3. Add sauce
  4. Add cheese
  5. Add any toppings
  6. Bake until cheese is melted and crust is well developed
  7. Enjoy!
